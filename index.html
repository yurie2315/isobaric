Isobaric Engineering Services

Isobaric Engineering Services is a multi-disciplinary Engineering and Construction firm specializing in Operation and Maintenance Services. The company is 100% owned and managed by Engr. Jim Arban and his team of highly experienced professionals and personnel emanating from engineering and construction background. The company separates itself from the rest with its professionalism and qualified team to undertake projects. We are dedicated to give you consistency, on-time-projects and outstanding services through engineering standards, principles and ethics.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and responsive Electro-Mechanical Engineering services.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering and construction company, renowned for our working methodologies, ,ethics, technological heritage, and human resource capabilities while attaining customer satisfaction.

Core Values
